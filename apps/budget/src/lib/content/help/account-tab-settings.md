# Settings Tab

Manage this account's configuration and properties.

## General Settings

### Account Name
Display name for the account throughout the app.

### Account Type
Classification affecting available features:
- Checking, Savings, Credit Card
- Investment, Loan, HSA, Other

### Currency
Primary currency for transactions and display.

### Notes
Personal notes or reminders about the account.

## Financial Settings

### Opening Balance
Starting balance when account was created.

### Current Balance
Calculated from transactions (read-only).

### Interest Rate
For debt accounts - used in payoff calculations.

### Credit Limit
For credit cards - enables utilization tracking.

## Data Management

### Export Transactions
Download transactions as CSV for backup or analysis.

### Merge Account
Combine with another account (advanced).

## Danger Zone

### Archive Account
Hide from main views but preserve history.

### Delete Account
Permanently remove account and all transactions.

## Tips

- Update settings when account details change
- Use notes for account numbers or reminders
- Export regularly for personal backup
- Archive unused accounts instead of deleting
