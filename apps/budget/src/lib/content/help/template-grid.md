# Template Cards

Each card represents a pre-configured budget template you can use as a starting point.

## Card Information

- **Icon** - Visual identifier for the budget type
- **Type Badge** - Shows the budget type:
  - **Account Monthly** - Tracks spending from a specific account
  - **Category Envelope** - Allocates money to a spending category
  - **Goal Based** - Savings targets with end dates
  - **Scheduled Expense** - Known recurring expenses
- **Suggested Amount** - A recommended monthly amount
- **Enforcement Level** - How strictly spending is limited

## How to Select

Click or tap any card to use that template. You'll be taken to the budget creation page with the template settings pre-filled.

## Tips

- Hover over cards to see a highlight effect
- All settings can be customized after selection
- Look at the enforcement level to understand how restrictive the budget will be
