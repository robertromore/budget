Schedules let you define recurring transactions that automatically appear in your account's transaction list.

## Creating a schedule

1. Navigate to **Schedules** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Schedule**
3. Enter the payee, amount, category, and linked account
4. Set the frequency (daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly)
5. Choose a start date and optional end date

## Frequency options

- **Daily** — Every N days
- **Weekly** — Specific days of the week, every N weeks
- **Monthly** — Specific day of the month, every N months
- **Yearly** — Specific month and day, every N years

## Auto-add

When **auto-add** is enabled, the app automatically creates transactions on their scheduled dates. This is useful for fixed bills like rent or subscriptions where the amount is consistent.

For variable amounts, you can set a **min/max range**. The app uses the average for projections and lets you adjust the actual amount when the transaction is created.

## Upcoming transactions

Scheduled transactions appear in your account's transaction list with a calendar icon. They show up to 30 days ahead, giving you a forecast of upcoming income and expenses.

## Tips

- Use schedules for all predictable transactions: rent, utilities, subscriptions, paychecks
- Enable auto-add for fixed-amount transactions to save time
- Use the amount range feature for bills that vary (electric, water)
- Review the **Upcoming** view tab on your account to see scheduled transactions
