# Theme Toggle

Switch between light and dark modes to match your preference or reduce eye strain.

## Available Themes

- **Light** - Bright interface for daytime use
- **Dark** - Reduced brightness for low-light environments
- **System** - Automatically matches your device settings

## Tips

- Dark mode can help reduce eye strain when working at night
- The theme preference is saved and persists across sessions
