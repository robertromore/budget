# Accounts Page

The Accounts page is your central hub for managing all financial accounts in your budget.

## Overview

This page displays all your accounts in a grid view, making it easy to see balances and access account details at a glance.

## Features

- **View all accounts** - See your checking, savings, credit cards, and other accounts
- **Quick balance overview** - Each card shows the current balance
- **Account details** - Account type, institution, and notes displayed on each card
- **Easy navigation** - Click any account to view its transactions

## Tips

- Accounts are color-coded with a left border for quick visual identification
- Use the "Seed Default Accounts" button to quickly set up common account types
- Closed accounts are marked with a badge for easy identification
