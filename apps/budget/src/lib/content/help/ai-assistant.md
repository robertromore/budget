# AI Assistant

The AI Assistant is your personal financial advisor, available to answer questions about your budget, spending, and finances.

## Opening the Chat

- Click the **sparkles** icon in the header
- Or press [[Ctrl+Shift+K]] / [[Cmd+Shift+K]]

## What You Can Ask

### Spending Questions

- "How much did I spend on groceries last month?"
- "What are my biggest expenses?"
- "Compare my spending this month vs last"

### Budget Help

- "Am I on track with my budget?"
- "Which categories am I overspending in?"
- "Suggest a budget for next month"

### Financial Insights

- "Find ways to save money"
- "What subscriptions am I paying for?"
- "Predict my cash flow for next month"

### Transaction Help

- "Categorize my uncategorized transactions"
- "Find duplicate transactions"
- "What did I spend at Amazon?"

## Slash Commands

Type `/` for quick commands:

- `/balance` - Check account balance
- `/budget` - View budget status
- `/savings` - Find savings opportunities
- `/help` - List all commands

## Features

- **Context-aware** - Knows which page you're on
- **Conversation history** - Remembers past chats
- **Real data** - Uses your actual financial data
- **Streaming** - See responses as they're generated

## Tips

- Be specific for better answers
- Ask follow-up questions
- Use slash commands for quick lookups
- Close panel with [[Escape]]
