# Quick Actions

The Quick Actions card provides convenient shortcuts to frequently used features and pages.

## Available Actions

- **Manage Accounts** - Navigate to the accounts list to view, edit, or add accounts
- **Manage Payees** - Access the payees section to organize your vendors and merchants
- **Manage Schedules** - Set up and modify recurring transactions
- **Payee Analytics** - View detailed insights and statistics about your payees

## Tips

- Use these shortcuts instead of navigating through the sidebar for faster access
- The analytics dashboard provides powerful insights into spending patterns
