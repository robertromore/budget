# Category Filter

Use these tabs to filter templates by category:

- **All** - Show all available templates
- **Bills** - Essential recurring expenses (rent, utilities, subscriptions)
- **Savings** - Goals and emergency funds
- **Lifestyle** - Entertainment, dining, hobbies
- **Transport** - Vehicle and commuting expenses

## Tips

- Categories help you find relevant templates quickly
- The count updates based on your search terms
- Click a category to see only templates in that area
