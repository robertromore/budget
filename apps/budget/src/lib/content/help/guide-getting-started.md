This guide walks you through setting up the app for the first time.

## Step 1: Create an account

Click **Add Account** in the sidebar or navigate to Accounts and click the plus icon. Choose the account type that matches your bank account (checking, savings, credit card, etc.) and enter a name.

Set the **initial balance** to your current account balance. This serves as the starting point for all running balance calculations.

## Step 2: Import transactions

Once your account is created, switch to the **Import** section. Upload a CSV, OFX, or QFX file exported from your bank. The import wizard walks you through column mapping, payee matching, and duplicate detection.

If you prefer manual entry, use the **Add Transaction** button to enter transactions one at a time.

## Step 3: Organize with categories

Transactions are organized by category (groceries, rent, utilities, etc.). The app suggests categories based on payee history. You can create custom categories under **Categories** in the sidebar.

## Step 4: Set up a budget

Navigate to **Budgets** and create your first budget. Choose a budget type (monthly account budget, category envelope, or goal-based) and set your target amounts.

## Step 5: Automate with schedules

For recurring transactions (rent, subscriptions, paychecks), create a **Schedule** to automatically generate upcoming transactions so you can plan ahead.

## Tips

- Import transactions regularly to keep your data current
- Use the **Analytics** section on each account for spending insights
- Set up **bank connections** in Settings for automatic sync
