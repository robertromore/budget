# Analytics Tab

Visualize your financial data with interactive charts and insights.

## Available Charts

### Monthly Spending
- Track expenses over time
- Compare month-to-month trends
- Identify seasonal patterns

### Income vs Expenses
- Side-by-side comparison
- Net savings calculation
- Trend analysis

### Top Categories
- Where your money goes
- Category breakdown by amount
- Percentage of total spending

### Top Payees
- Highest spending merchants
- Transaction frequency
- Average transaction size

## Chart Controls

- **Time Range** - Select date range to analyze
- **Chart Type** - Switch between chart styles
- **Grouping** - Group by week, month, quarter, or year

## Debt Account View

For credit cards and loans:
- Payment progress tracking
- Interest calculations
- Payoff projections

## Tips

- Hover over chart elements for details
- Click segments to drill down
- Export charts as images
- Compare periods using date range selector

## Related Features

- Intelligence tab for AI-powered insights
- Budget tracking for spending limits
- Transaction table for detailed data
