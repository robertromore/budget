Subscriptions can quietly consume a significant portion of your budget. The app helps you identify, track, and manage all recurring charges.

## Finding your subscriptions

The **Subscriptions** section (accessible from the sidebar or within each account) automatically detects recurring charges by analyzing your transaction patterns. It identifies:

- Monthly subscriptions (streaming, software, memberships)
- Annual renewals (domains, insurance, professional dues)
- Irregular recurring charges (quarterly, semi-annual)

## Managing subscriptions

For each detected subscription, you can see:

- **Amount** and frequency
- **Next expected charge** date
- **Total annual cost** — the yearly impact of each subscription
- **History** — past charges and any price changes

## Reducing subscription costs

Review your subscription list monthly and ask:

1. **Am I using this?** Cancel anything untouched in the past month
2. **Is there a cheaper alternative?** Compare plans and providers
3. **Can I share?** Family plans for streaming services save money
4. **Annual vs. monthly?** Annual billing typically offers 10-20% savings

## Setting up schedule tracking

For subscriptions not automatically detected, create a **schedule** manually:

1. Set the payee, amount, and category
2. Choose the billing frequency (monthly, yearly, etc.)
3. Enable **auto-add** for fixed amounts

This ensures upcoming charges appear in your transaction forecast.

## Tips

- Tag subscription transactions with a dedicated "Subscriptions" category for easy filtering
- Review the total annual cost of all subscriptions together — small monthly charges add up
- Set calendar reminders before free trials end
- Check for price increases by comparing current charges to the subscription history
