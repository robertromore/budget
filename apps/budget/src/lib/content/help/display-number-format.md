# Number Format

Choose how numbers are formatted based on your locale.

## Available Formats

- **English (US)** - 1,234.56 (comma thousands, period decimal)
- **German** - 1.234,56 (period thousands, comma decimal)
- **French** - 1 234,56 (space thousands, comma decimal)

## What It Affects

- Thousands separator character
- Decimal separator character
- Display of all monetary amounts

## Tips

- Choose the format common in your region
- This affects how numbers are displayed, not how they're stored
- Important for correctly reading imported CSV files
