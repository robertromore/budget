# Medical Expenses Tab

Specialized tracking for Health Savings Account (HSA) medical expenses.

## Purpose

Track all medical expenses that can be reimbursed from your HSA:
- Doctor visits and copays
- Prescriptions and medications
- Medical equipment and supplies
- Vision and dental expenses
- Therapy and treatments

## Expense Table

View all recorded medical expenses:
- **Date** - When the expense occurred
- **Provider** - Doctor, pharmacy, or facility
- **Type** - Category of medical expense
- **Amount** - Cost of the service
- **Reimbursed** - Whether paid from HSA

## Adding Expenses

1. Click "Add Expense"
2. Enter provider and amount
3. Select expense type
4. Attach receipt (optional)
5. Mark reimbursement status

## Expense Types

- Office Visits
- Prescriptions
- Lab Work
- Medical Equipment
- Vision Care
- Dental Care
- Mental Health
- Other Qualified Expenses

## Reimbursement Tracking

Track which expenses have been:
- Paid directly from HSA
- Reimbursed from HSA
- Pending reimbursement
- Paid out-of-pocket (for future reimbursement)

## Tips

- Keep receipts for all medical expenses
- Track out-of-pocket expenses for future reimbursement
- Review IRS guidelines for eligible expenses
- Consider tax implications of reimbursement timing
