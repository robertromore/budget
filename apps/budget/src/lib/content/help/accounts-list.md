# Accounts List

Your accounts represent different financial holdings like checking, savings, credit cards, or investment accounts.

## Account Types

- **Checking** - Day-to-day spending accounts
- **Savings** - Money set aside for future use
- **Credit Card** - Credit accounts with balances owed
- **Investment** - Brokerage and retirement accounts
- **HSA** - Health Savings Accounts with special tracking

## Actions

- Click an account to view its transactions
- Use the settings tab to modify account details
- Import transactions from bank exports

## Tips

- Account balances update automatically when you add transactions
- Use account groups to organize related accounts
