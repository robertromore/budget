# Intelligence Dashboard

The Intelligence page provides AI-powered insights and machine learning features to help you understand your finances better.

## Features

### Natural Language Search
Search your transactions using plain English queries like:
- "Show me coffee purchases last month"
- "Large transactions over $500"
- "Subscriptions I haven't used recently"

### ML Health Overview
Monitor the status of machine learning services and their accuracy.

### Quick Insights
- **Income vs Expenses** - Compare monthly income and spending
- **Budget Risk** - See budgets at risk of overspending
- **Savings Opportunities** - Discover potential savings

### Forecasting
30-day cash flow predictions based on your spending patterns.

### Anomaly Detection
Automatic detection of unusual transactions that may need review.

### Pattern Detection
- **Recurring Patterns** - Auto-detected subscriptions and bills
- **Payee Matching** - Find and merge duplicate payees

## Available Actions

- **Retrain Models** - Update ML models with latest data
- **Settings** - Configure AI/ML preferences

## Tips

- Use natural language search for quick transaction lookups
- Review anomalies weekly to catch unusual charges
- Check savings opportunities for budget optimization ideas
