# Analytics Tab

Interactive charts and visualizations for understanding your financial patterns.

## Available Charts

### Spending Over Time
- Monthly expense trends
- Year-over-year comparisons
- Seasonal pattern detection

### Income vs Expenses
- Net savings visualization
- Cash flow analysis
- Balance projections

### Category Breakdown
- Pie/donut charts by category
- Spending distribution
- Budget comparison

### Top Payees
- Highest spending merchants
- Transaction frequency analysis
- Average transaction amounts

## Chart Controls

- **Date Range** - Adjust time period
- **Chart Type** - Switch visualization styles
- **Grouping** - Week, month, quarter, or year

## For Debt Accounts

Credit cards and loans show:
- Payment progress tracking
- Interest accumulation
- Payoff timeline projections

## Tips

- Hover for detailed tooltips
- Click segments to drill down
- Compare different time periods
- Export charts for reporting
