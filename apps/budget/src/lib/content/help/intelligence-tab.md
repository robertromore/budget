# Intelligence Tab

The Intelligence tab provides machine learning-powered insights about your financial data.

## Features

### Income vs Expenses
Compare your income and expenses month-over-month. Track trends and identify changes in your financial patterns.

### Budget Risk Assessment
Identify budgets that may exceed their limits based on current spending patterns. Get early warnings to adjust spending.

### Cash Flow Forecast
Predict future account balances based on historical patterns and recurring transactions. Plan ahead with confidence.

### Anomaly Detection
Automatically identify unusual transactions that may need review. Catch errors or fraud early.

## Tips

- Review forecasts regularly to stay ahead of potential cash flow issues
- Act on budget risk warnings before overspending occurs
- Investigate flagged anomalies promptly for security
