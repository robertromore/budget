# Budget Tabs

The tabs provide access to different budget management features.

## Available Tabs

### Budget Overview
The main list of all your budgets with current status, spending progress, and quick actions.

### Recommendations
AI-powered budget suggestions based on your spending patterns. Accept or dismiss recommendations to help build better budgets.

### Groups
Organize budgets into logical groups (e.g., "Essentials", "Fun Money", "Savings Goals") for better organization.

### Fund Transfer
Move allocated funds between budgets. Useful when one category needs more than originally planned.

### Rollover Manager
Manage how unused budget amounts carry forward to the next period. Configure rollover rules for each budget.

### Analytics & Insights
Detailed charts and analysis of your budget performance:
- Spending trends over time
- Category breakdowns
- Forecast projections
- Historical comparisons

## Tips

- Check Recommendations periodically for budget optimization ideas
- Use Groups to organize budgets by priority or purpose
- Review Analytics monthly to identify spending patterns
