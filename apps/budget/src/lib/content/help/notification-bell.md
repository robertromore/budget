Click the bell icon to view recent notifications. A badge appears when there are unread items.

## Notification Types

- **Success** - Confirmations for completed actions (saved, deleted, imported)
- **Error** - Alerts when something goes wrong
- **Info** - General status updates and tips

## Actions

- **Mark all as read** - Clear the unread badge
- **Dismiss** - Remove individual notifications
- **Clear all** - Remove all notifications from the list

## Settings

Notification behavior can be customized in **Settings > Display**:

- **Notification Style** - Choose between toast popups, the notification center, or both
- **Notification Verbosity** - Control which types of notifications are shown
