# Transaction Entry Methods

Choose the best method for entering your transaction.

## Manual Entry

Traditional form-based entry with full control over all fields.

**Best for:**
- Precise control over all details
- Quick entry when you know all values
- Power users familiar with the form

**Fields available:**
- Amount, Date, Payee, Category
- Budget allocation, Notes

## Transfer

Simplified form for moving money between your accounts.

**Best for:**
- Account-to-account transfers
- Moving money to savings
- Credit card payments

**Creates:**
- Matching transactions in both accounts
- Linked for easy tracking

## Guided (Wizard)

Step-by-step process with smart suggestions.

**Best for:**
- New users learning the system
- When you need guidance
- Complex transactions

**Features:**
- One question at a time
- Smart suggestions at each step
- Validation before proceeding
- Can be cancelled at any step
