Use the dropdown selector to switch between different views for your account.

## Available Sections

- **Transactions** - View and manage all account transactions
- **Analytics** - Charts and trends for spending, income, and balances
- **Subscriptions** - Track recurring charges and subscriptions
- **Intelligence** - AI-powered insights and predictions
- **Automation** - Rule-based automation for categorization and actions
- **Schedules** - Manage recurring transaction schedules
- **Budgets** - Budget tracking and allocation for this account
- **Documents** - Attach and manage account-related documents
- **Import** - Import transactions from bank files (CSV, OFX, QFX)
- **Settings** - Configure account preferences and financial details

Some account types show additional sections (e.g., Medical Expenses and HSA Dashboard for HSA accounts, Usage for utility accounts).
