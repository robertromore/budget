# Quick Add Transaction

Add new transactions to your accounts with a streamlined form.

## Required Fields

- **Date** - When the transaction occurred
- **Amount** - The transaction value (positive or negative)
- **Description** - A brief note about the transaction

## Optional Fields

- **Payee** - The merchant or vendor
- **Category** - Budget category for tracking
- **Memo** - Additional notes or references

## Tips

- Use negative amounts for expenses and positive for income
- The category field auto-suggests based on the payee
- Recent payees appear at the top of the suggestions
