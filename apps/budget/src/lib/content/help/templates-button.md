# Templates Button

Click this button to browse and select from pre-built budget templates.

## What are Templates?

Templates are pre-configured budget setups for common scenarios:
- Monthly bills and subscriptions
- Savings goals
- Lifestyle spending categories
- Transportation costs

## Benefits

- **Quick setup** - Start with sensible defaults
- **Best practices** - Templates follow common budgeting patterns
- **Customizable** - Adjust any setting after selecting a template

Click this button to open the template picker and explore your options.
