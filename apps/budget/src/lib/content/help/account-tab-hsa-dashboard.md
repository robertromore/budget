# HSA Dashboard Tab

Comprehensive overview of your Health Savings Account status.

## Account Summary

### Current Balance
Available funds for medical expenses.

### Contribution Status
- YTD contributions
- Annual limit progress
- Employer contributions

### Investment Balance
For HSA accounts with investment options.

## Expense Summary

### This Year
- Total medical expenses
- Reimbursed amount
- Unreimbursed eligible expenses

### Lifetime
- Historical expense tracking
- Cumulative savings

## Key Metrics

### Contribution Room
How much more you can contribute this year.

### Unreimbursed Balance
Out-of-pocket expenses eligible for future reimbursement.

### Effective Savings
Tax savings from HSA contributions.

## Visualizations

- Contribution progress chart
- Expense breakdown by category
- Balance over time
- Provider spending analysis

## HSA Benefits Reminder

- **Triple Tax Advantage**
  - Tax-deductible contributions
  - Tax-free growth
  - Tax-free qualified withdrawals

## Tips

- Maximize annual contributions
- Consider investing unused balance
- Keep all medical receipts indefinitely
- Review contribution limits annually
- Track eligible expenses even if not reimbursing immediately
