# Financial Overview

The Financial Overview section provides quick summaries of your accounts, recent payees, and active schedules without leaving the dashboard.

## Sections

### Account Summary
Shows your top 5 accounts with their current balances. Positive balances are shown in green, negative in red.

### Recent Payees
Lists your most recent payees with quick links to their analytics. This helps you track who you've been transacting with most recently.

### Active Schedules
Displays your scheduled recurring transactions and their current status (active, paused, etc.).

## Tips

- Click any payee's "Analytics" button to see detailed transaction history and patterns
- If you have more items than shown, a count indicator will let you know how many more exist
- Navigate to the full section pages for complete lists and management options
