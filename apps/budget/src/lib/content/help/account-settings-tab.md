# Account Settings

Configure preferences and manage data for this account.

## Settings Sections

### General Settings
- **Account Name** - Display name for the account
- **Account Type** - Checking, savings, credit card, etc.
- **Currency** - Currency for transactions
- **Institution** - Bank or financial institution

### Financial Settings
- **Current Balance** - Adjust the account balance
- **Credit Limit** - For credit cards
- **Interest Rate** - For loans and credit cards

### Data Management
- **Export** - Download transactions as CSV
- **Merge** - Combine with another account
- **Archive** - Hide from active view

### Danger Zone
- **Delete Account** - Permanently remove account and all transactions

## Tips

- Changes take effect immediately
- Balance adjustments create adjustment transactions
- Archived accounts can be restored
- Deleted accounts cannot be recovered

## Account Types

| Type | Features |
|------|----------|
| Checking | Standard transactions |
| Savings | Interest tracking |
| Credit Card | Credit limit, payment tracking |
| Cash | Simple expense tracking |
| Investment | Holdings and returns |
| HSA | Medical expense tracking |
| Loan | Payoff tracking |
