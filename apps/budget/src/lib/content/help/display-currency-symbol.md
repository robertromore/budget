# Currency Symbol

Set the currency symbol displayed before monetary values.

## Common Symbols

- **$** - US Dollar, Canadian Dollar, Australian Dollar
- **€** - Euro
- **£** - British Pound
- **¥** - Japanese Yen, Chinese Yuan

## How It Works

The symbol appears before all currency amounts throughout the app. You can enter any text, including multi-character currency codes like "USD" or "EUR".

## Tips

- Enter just the symbol for a cleaner look
- The preview shows how amounts will appear
- Changes apply immediately across the app
