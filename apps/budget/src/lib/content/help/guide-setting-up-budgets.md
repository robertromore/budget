Budgets help you plan spending and track progress against your goals.

## Budget types

### Account monthly

Set a total spending limit for a specific account each month. Good for controlling overall spending on a checking account or credit card.

### Category envelope

Allocate specific amounts to individual categories (groceries, dining, entertainment). Unspent amounts can roll over to the next period. This follows the envelope budgeting methodology.

### Goal-based

Track progress toward a savings target. Link the budget to an account and set a target amount and optional deadline. The app calculates your progress automatically.

## Creating a budget

1. Navigate to **Budgets** in the sidebar
2. Click **Create Budget** or choose from templates
3. Select the budget type, period, and target amount
4. Link it to an account and optionally assign categories
5. Choose an enforcement level (none, warning, or strict)

## Using templates

The template picker offers pre-built budgets for common scenarios. Browse by category or search for a specific template. Templates pre-fill the budget type, categories, and suggested amounts.

## Monitoring progress

Each budget shows a progress bar with current spending vs. your target. The color changes as you approach or exceed the limit:

- **Green** — Under 75% of budget
- **Yellow** — Between 75% and 100%
- **Red** — Over budget

## Tips

- Start with a few broad category budgets and refine over time
- Use the **Recommendations** feature for AI-suggested budget allocations
- Review budget performance in the **Analytics** section
